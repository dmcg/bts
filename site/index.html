Between July and October 2016 I worked for Beyond the Story Ltd. One of my invoices was paid, two remain unpaid.

After 2 months of broken promises to pay, I presented a Statutory Demand for the money. When I tried to take this demand to the County Court I was threatened with an injunction by their lawyer. He did not dispute the debt, but complained that the Statutory Demand was served personally on the managing director not at their registered office, but in the next door office.

Neither Beyond the Story Ltd nor their lawyer dispute the debt - I have a signed undertaking to pay the amount total plus interest, by the end of January. Despite this I have not received the money, and they continue to trade.
